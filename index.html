FAQ

Questions people ask

What does AI process automation mean here?

It’s the layer that combines CRM data, workflow logic, and AI assistants so repetitive work, QA, or routing decisions happen automatically—but every action is documented and easy to edit.

Which CRMs and tools do you work with?

HubSpot is the primary build environment, but I adapt to similar CRMs. Automations rely on Zapier/Make, APIs, Slack, Google Workspace, and lightweight LLM workflows.

Can you integrate my existing stack?

Yes. We audit your current stack, map data owners, and use APIs or middleware to make sure marketing, sales, and ops teams share the same source of truth.

How fast can we launch something useful?

Most projects ship an initial CRM + automation layer within a few weeks, with AI workflows layered on immediately after sign-off. Timelines depend on access and approvals.

Do you need cookies or trackers?

No. This page runs without tracking pixels, and I prioritize privacy-first data capture. If analytics are needed, we scope them with compliance in mind.

What do you need from us to start?

Calendar access for a kickoff, admin rights to your CRM and automations, a short list of goals, and any compliance notes so the build aligns with your policies.
