The problem

More ad spend wasn't the answer

Premium dental clinics running solid campaigns still lose a large share of inbound leads before they ever sit in the chair. The bottlenecks are predictable:

  • Slow first response — leads go cold in under five minutes
  • No consistent follow-up after the first message
  • High no-show rates with no automated reminder system
  • No visibility into where the pipeline is actually leaking

The clinic wasn't short on demand. It was short on system. Leads were captured manually via WhatsApp and a spreadsheet. That's the gap this project closes.

Context

Clinic profile

  • Type: Premium dental clinic, Madrid
  • Average ticket: €2,500–€5,000 per treatment
  • Lead sources: Google Ads, Meta Ads, web form, WhatsApp
  • Team: 3–5 front-desk and ops staff
  • Starting point: No CRM — leads managed via WhatsApp and a spreadsheet

What I built

The complete system — from lead to signed treatment

A single, connected automation layer that handles every step from inbound lead to confirmed appointment, with full pipeline visibility at each stage.

Automated lead capture

All inbound channels — web form, Meta Ads, Google Ads, and WhatsApp — route into a single HubSpot pipeline. No lead falls through the cracks.

Sub-1-minute first response

A Make automation triggers an instant WhatsApp message the moment a lead is created — before any human has touched it.

Lead scoring & qualification

Contacts are scored based on channel, engagement, and intent signals. Leads reaching ≥70 points automatically trigger the booking sequence.

Booking & confirmation flow

Qualified leads receive a HubSpot Meetings link. On booking, a deal is created and automatic confirmations go out via WhatsApp and email.

Reminders & no-show recovery

Automated reminders at 24h and 2h before the appointment. No-shows trigger a reactivation flow with two attempts within 72 hours.

Dashboard & reporting

A Looker Studio dashboard tracks conversion at every pipeline stage — show rates, time-to-close, CAC estimates, and revenue by channel.

The pipeline

8 stages, fully automated transitions

Every stage has a clear entry and exit criterion — so the team always knows what action to take next, and the system handles the rest.

  1. 01New lead — Form / WhatsApp / Ad received. Auto-response in <1 min.
  2. 02Contacted — First contact made. Follow-up sequence starts.
  3. 03Qualified — Lead Score ≥70. Booking sequence triggered.
  4. 04Appointment scheduled — Booking confirmed. Deal created + reminders sent.
  5. 05Appointment completed — Patient attended. Quote task assigned to team.
  6. 06Quote sent — Proposal delivered. 48h follow-up task created.
  7. 07Closed won — Accepted + paid. Satisfaction survey sent automatically.
  8. 08Closed lost — No-show ×2 or declined. 30-day reactivation workflow begins.
